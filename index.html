The Regional Centre for Geodesy (RCG) at IIT Bombay stands as a key hub for advanced research, education, and innovation in the dynamic and evolving field of Geodesy. The centre is dedicated to deepening our understanding of the Earthâ€™s shape, gravity field, and rotational behavior, along with their changes over time. Using advanced technologies such as GNSS, Satellite Altimetry, and Remote Sensing, RCG addresses critical challenges in areas like crustal deformation monitoring, sea level variations, precise geospatial positioning, and infrastructure development.

RCG is committed to fostering a vibrant academic and research environment that supports the development of future geodetic scientists and engineers. Through a mix of training programs, hands-on learning, and academic engagement, the centre encourages innovation and inquiry. By collaborating with national and international organizations, RCG strives to transform cutting-edge research into meaningful solutions that address real-world problems and contribute to both scientific progress and societal benefit.
